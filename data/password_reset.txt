Password Reset Guide

Issue: User unable to login or forgot password.

Steps:
1. Go to login page
2. Click on "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your registered email ID
4. Check your email inbox for reset link
5. Click the link and set a new password
6. Reset link expires in 10 minutes for security reasons

If email not received:
- Check spam folder
- Ensure email is correct
- Wait 2–3 minutes before retry
